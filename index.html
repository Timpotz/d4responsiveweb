Lilith

Lilith was the daughter of Mephisto and the sister of Lucion. She was known to all of Sanctuary as the Queen of the Succubi. Once the lover of the archangel Inarius, her union with him led to the creation of the first nephalem.

The Butcher

A bloated and grotesque creature,The Butcher was a sadistic being that relished in the torture and pain of others. It was also masochistic, and its own hands were damaged as the result of its torture. It hunted endlessly for fresh meat, apparently preferring it raw.
